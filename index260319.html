Privacy Policy

IQRBlock: 6-in-1 Block Puzzle

Effective date: 2026-03-16

IQRBlock ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user and device data.

1. Who this app is for

IQRBlock is designed for children and families, including users under 13.

2. Data we collect

  • We do not require account registration.
  • We do not ask for name, email, phone number, or home address in the app.
  • If you choose photos from your device, images are used only to create puzzle content in the app.

3. Photo access and use

  • The app uses the system image picker to let you select photos.
  • Selected photos are processed (including square crop) for gameplay.
  • Photos are stored locally on your device/app storage for puzzle use.
  • We do not sell your photos.
  • We do not upload your photos to our server for current core gameplay.

4. Ads and third-party services

IQRBlock uses Google AdMob for ads (banner/interstitial/rewarded).

AdMob may process limited device data (for example, advertising identifiers, device/app information, and approximate technical data) according to Google's policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's privacy

Because this app may be used by children, we configure ad requests with child-directed settings where applicable.

Parents/guardians can supervise photo selection and app usage.

6. Data sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We only share data with service providers needed to run app features (for example, ad delivery), under their own privacy policies.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Puzzle photos you import are kept in local app storage unless you remove them.
  • You can delete photos from "My Gallery" inside the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes local app data according to Android system behavior.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this URL.
